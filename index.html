About Us

FOUNDER

Mr Krishanapal

Founded by Krishanapal Singh, the Bahuripar Library stands as a beacon of knowledge and learning for the local community. Opened on July 22, this modern library aims to provide accessible educational resources to the residents of Gram Bahuripar and surrounding areas. With a diverse collection of books, magazines, and digital resources, it caters to learners of all ages, from students to lifelong readers. The library's mission is to foster a culture of reading and intellectual growth while promoting community engagement. It serves as a platform for educational events, literacy programs.
